Before doing anything, Claude builds a picture of your project. It searches the
codebase for relevant files, reads them, and checks your git state: current
branch, uncommitted changes, recent commits.

A simple question about your code might stop right here. Claude reads enough to
answer, then replies. Nothing is edited during this phase.

It is the same legwork you would do by hand in a chat, except Claude does it
itself and decides what is worth reading based on your goal.
