After acting, Claude reads what came back: test output, error messages, the diff
it just made. It decides whether the step worked and what to do next.

If the tests still fail, it loops back to gather more context and tries again,
course-correcting from what it just learned.

This self-checking is why an agent can handle multi-step work that would take
many chat round trips. It closes the feedback loop itself instead of handing
each result back to you.
