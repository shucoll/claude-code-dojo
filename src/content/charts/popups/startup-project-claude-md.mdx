Your project's conventions, build commands, and architecture notes, loaded in
full at the start of every conversation and shared with everyone who clones the
repo.

At 1,800 tokens it is the biggest line item you actually control, and the one
that grows without anyone deciding to grow it. Every line you add is a line that
loads while you are doing unrelated work.

The docs put the target at **under 200 lines**. Past that, the fix is not to
delete your conventions but to move them somewhere that loads on demand:
path-scoped rules in `.claude/rules/` that load only when a matching file is
touched, or a skill that loads only when invoked.
